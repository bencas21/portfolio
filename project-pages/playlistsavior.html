Project Details / Background
Introducing PlaylistSavior, a cutting-edge Flask web application designed to turn your music tastes into tailor-made playlists with just a few words. Powered by a large language model via Ollama, PlaylistSavior effortlessly transforms your natural language requests into Spotify API calls, curating personalized playlists from a massive library of over 100 million songs. Whether you're in the mood for a chill study session, a high-energy workout, or a nostalgic throwback, PlaylistSavior delivers up to 100 tracks in under 5 seconds, ensuring you always have the perfect soundtrack.
As I'm still in the early stages of the app's development, you'll need to contact me directly to get access. For now, I'm manually entering users into the Spotify API to ensure everything runs smoothly while I work on refining the experience. So, if you're eager to give PlaylistSavior a spin and explore its magic, reach out, and I'll get you set up!
